Facilities & Amenities

Tough on the outside, sweet on the inside

At HotelMOTEL, hotel luxury meets motel affordability and convenience, all in the heart of the Adelaide CBD. Book your stay with us and you’ll enjoy access to our extensive facilities and services too.

Reception

The 24hour reception at HotelMOTEL means you can arrive in your own time.

Wi-Fi

FREE as who pays for Wi-Fi these days

Restaurant

There’s always space at the table for HotelMOTEL guests at our adjoining property, The Terrace Hotel. Drop by breakfast on-the-go or a sit-down dinner, all served with a dash of good old fashioned service.

In room eats

Stay in and eat with in room delivery straight to your motel room door. Order through your in room QR code and enjoy a range of street food inspired snacks and meals, all with our HotelMOTEL spin.

Laundry

Make use of the laundry services at The Terrace Hotel and head home with a suitcase of clothes smelling fresh as a daisy. Charges apply, please contact the reception for rates.

Health & wellbeing

HotelMOTEL understands the importance of wellbeing, health and fitness while travelling.

Gym, Pool & Spa

With exclusive access to neighbouring Next Gen Health & Lifestyle Club, you are sure to stay relaxed, invigorated and perfectly balanced during your time with us. Work off jet lag or train for another personal best at Next Gen’s premium recreation facilities, including a well-equipped gym, tennis and squash courts, heated indoor and outdoor pools and spa.

Outdoor Fitness

Make the most of HotelMOTEL’s stunning location by exploring the many walking and running trails along the banks of the River Torrens or throughout the surrounding parklands. You will also find outdoor gym equipment along these trails, as well as a number of local bootcamps, personal training sessions and group fitness classes. Our guest services team will be happy to point you in the right direction or enrol you in a local class.

Beauty & Wellness

Unwind and experience total relaxation with a personal beauty or massage package at one of Adelaide’s top day spas, arranged just for you by our concierge. Or if you simply can’t leave the comfort of your room, ask about our range of in-room massage treatments. Fitness facilities are available at no charge and exclusively to all HotelMOTEL guests, while day spa, massage or beauty treatments will be quoted according to your needs.

Parking services & transfers

HotelMOTEL offers onsite valet car parking and secure self-parking options, as well as our premium Adelaide Airport transfer service. Additional charges apply for all of these services.

HotelMOTEL Entrance

Guests are invited to arrive via the underground carpark from King William Road, or on foot via the eastern promenade on ground level before ascending to our floating reception space on level three.

Transfers

Book our door-to-door airport transfer service to be greeted at Adelaide Airport by our professional chauffeur, who will drive you to Oval Hotel in the comfort and style of a high-class vehicle. Sit back, relax and enjoy free wi-fi, bottled water and mints.

Parking Services

Driving to Oval Hotel? We offer a range of secure, onsite parking options 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. These are accessible via our main entrance on King William Road.

Valet Parking is available for $45 AUD per 24 hours (7 days a week, subject to availability)

Self-parking is available and payable directly to Wilson Parking at the payment terminal on departure. Subject to availability, associated rates per entry/exit apply.

Event Day Parking

Please note, on Adelaide Oval event days HotelMOTEL is only able to provide our valet service.
